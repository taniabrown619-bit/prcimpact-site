1
Government · Education · Alabama
Securing $500 Million for Montgomery Public Schools

Montgomery had gone years without meaningful investment in public school infrastructure. Families were navigating aging facilities, outdated resources, and persistent equity concerns. School tax measures are notoriously difficult to pass in Alabama, where voter skepticism, misinformation, and distrust of institutions often create a steep uphill climb. It passed.

2
Public Safety · Corporate Partnership · Alabama
Supporting a Public-Private Safety Initiative for Domestic Violence Survivors

Domestic violence survivors often face ongoing safety risks even after leaving an abusive situation. A partnership involving Ring, Amazon, the City of Montgomery, and One Place Family Justice Center aimed to provide survivors with added security through donated doorbell and camera devices. PRC Impact provided research and advisory support to help the initiative reach and serve survivors effectively.

3
Public Safety · Municipal Government · Alabama
Informing Birmingham's Violence Prevention Strategy Through Youth and Community Research

The City of Birmingham needed direct input from youth, families, and community stakeholders to understand what they believed was driving violence, what support systems were missing, and what prevention strategies would matter most in their communities. PRC Impact designed and conducted qualitative research that shaped the city's approach to community violence prevention.

4
Healthcare · Qualitative Research · New York
Clarifying Prescriber Understanding Through FDA-Related Qualitative Research

When new prescribing information is introduced, healthcare providers are expected to interpret it quickly and apply it accurately. But even well-structured materials can be misunderstood or filtered through prior prescribing habits. PRC Impact conducted 32 in-depth interviews with clinicians across five medical specialties. Findings directly informed updated federal prescribing guidelines.
