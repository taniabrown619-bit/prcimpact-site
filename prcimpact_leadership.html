Tania Johns
Founder & Principal, PRC Impact

Before founding PRC Impact, Tania Johns built a career at the intersection of public policy, qualitative research, and government strategy, advising leaders, shaping campaigns, and conducting research that directly informed consequential decisions across government and industry.

She launched PRC Impact around a single conviction: strategy without research is just opinion. Her work now sits at the intersection of AI governance, compliance architecture, qualitative research, and public policy, and she brings the same rigor to all of it.

She works with clients nationwide.

Her Approach

Direct, rigorous, and deeply practical.

Tania's work is shaped by years of operating in high-stakes environments where the research had to hold up under scrutiny, the strategy had to survive contact with real stakeholders, and the advice had to be something a decision-maker could actually act on.

She stays close to the work, not as an advisor who disappears after the deck, but as a partner invested in what actually happens next. That means understanding the political environment, the internal culture, and the people who will be most affected by the decisions being made.

That discipline runs through every engagement, whether the work is AI governance, qualitative research, compliance architecture, or helping an organization think through how to bring its people along.
