Why This Matters

Most organizations don't have an AI problem. They have a people problem that AI has made more urgent.

PRC Impact brings the governance expertise and the stakeholder insight to address both.

What We Do

We help organizations put the right structure around AI adoption.

1
AI Governance & Readiness

We help organizations establish the foundation for responsible AI adoption — governance structure, internal policy, staff readiness, training strategy, and rollout planning. Designed for high-accountability environments where accountability, trust, and decision-making process matter. We understand how these organizations work, how decisions move through them, and what it takes to bring people along.

2
Compliance Architecture & Custom Tool Design

For organizations that need more than policy on paper. We design internal AI compliance tools and governance infrastructure built to your specific environment — including audit trail management, role-based access controls, departmental workflow documentation, and compliance reporting your board, funders, and legal team can stand behind. Built for organizations in regulated environments — including healthcare, managed care, and government — who need AI governance that is documented, structured, and defensible.

3
Stakeholder Research & Implementation Strategy

Before leaders set policy or roll out new tools, they need to understand how people will respond and how to bring them along. We design focus groups, in-depth interviews, and listening sessions that surface concerns early, then help organizations think through the communication and alignment strategy to make adoption stick. RIVA-certified moderation. Research that has informed government policy and federal guidelines.
