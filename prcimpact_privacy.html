Who We Are

PRC Impact is an AI governance, policy, and qualitative research consulting firm based in the United States. We work with government agencies, nonprofit organizations, school systems, foundations, and public institutions. Our website is located at prcimpact.com.

Questions about this policy can be directed to us at hello@prcimpact.com.

Information you provide: When you schedule a connection call, submit a contact form, or communicate with us by email, we collect your name, email address, organization name, and any other information you choose to share.

Information collected automatically: When you visit prcimpact.com, we may collect standard web analytics data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs. We use this information to understand how visitors use our site and to improve it.
